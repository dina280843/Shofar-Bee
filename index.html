How to Play
  • Every word must contain the center letter L
  • Minimum 4 letters — no maximum
  • No proper nouns
  • Yiddish words welcome — shlep, chutzpah and friends! 🙂
  • Find the Pangram using all 7 letters for ⭐ top honours
  • Tap letters or type on keyboard · Backspace to delete
  • Post your words in the Substack comments
About the Puzzle

A word game rooted in Jerusalem

The Shofar Bee is a daily word puzzle created by Dina Raveh and Shalom Brenner in Jerusalem. Each puzzle is inspired by Israeli culture, history, and current events — from museum exhibitions to archaeological discoveries to the textures of everyday life in the city.

Every day, find as many words as you can from 7 letters. Post your discoveries in the Substack comments — Reader Discoveries published the following day.
